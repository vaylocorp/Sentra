## 🎯 Purpose

**Sentra** is a modern, reliable **endpoint monitoring platform** designed to protect your production services from unexpected downtime.

It continuously checks your HTTP endpoints — APIs, health checks, webhooks, GraphQL endpoints, and more — **every 60 seconds** from multiple locations around the world.  

When something goes wrong, Sentra notifies you **instantly**. When your service recovers, it sends a recovery alert so you know everything is back to normal.  

No complicated agents to install. No heavy configuration. Just add a URL and Sentra starts working immediately.

Sentra gives developers and teams the confidence that their critical services are being watched 24/7, so they can focus on building instead of firefighting.

## ✨ Core Capabilities

### ⚡ Ultra-Fast & Reliable Monitoring
- Checks every **60 seconds** — maximum ~60 seconds of unknown downtime
- Global monitoring powered by **Vercel Edge** for fast and consistent results
- Instant failure detection and **recovery notifications**

### 📊 Performance & Insights
- Tracks response time and latency trends over time
- Detailed incident history and uptime statistics
- Clear visualizations to understand how your services are performing

### 🌐 Universal Compatibility
- Works with **any HTTP endpoint**
- Supports REST APIs, GraphQL, health-check routes, webhooks, static sites, and more
- No limitations on response types or payload size

### 🔒 Privacy & Data Handling
We store your monitored URLs and check logs **only** for the purpose of performing the monitoring, maintaining accurate history, and delivering timely alerts.  
All your data is protected with **row-level security**, ensuring complete isolation between users. Your monitoring data is never shared or visible to anyone else.

### 👥 Collaboration Ready
Invite team members to monitor and manage endpoints together (available on Pro and Enterprise plans).

## 🛠️ How It Works

Getting started with Sentra is incredibly simple:

1. **Sign up for free** at [sentra-theta.vercel.app](https://sentra-theta.vercel.app/login)
2. **Add your endpoint** — Paste any URL you want to monitor (example: `https://api.yourapp.com/health`)
3. **Choose your check settings** — Optional advanced options if needed
4. **Sit back and relax** — Sentra starts checking globally every 60 seconds

You’ll receive alerts via email by default. Paid plans unlock push notifications, Slack, and webhook integrations.

**From the moment you add your first endpoint, Sentra is live in under 2 minutes.**
